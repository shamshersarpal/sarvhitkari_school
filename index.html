Message From Principal

In this modern era of cut throat competition, whenever we talk about the aim of education in general, we come across innumerable questions, about what our modern schools are up to. The standardized responses or answers, we get or perceive, are more or less philosophical hearsays, depicting ideologies avoiding practicalities, making education system itself complicated. In the whole process an un-ignorable point is quite-evident, that students are turned into machines, bereft sentiments and human values. Earning stacks of money, to live a life of comforts materialistically, is not a bad idea, but with this; spiritual enlightenment of an individual is quintessential.

Keeping this in view, Jitender Veer Sarvhitkari Model Sr. Sec. School, since its inception has been running from pillar to post, to set benchmarks not only in academics, but in inculcating moral ethics amongst its pupils, so as to transform today’s young minds into corner stones of an ideal social structure of posterity. Jitender Veer Sarvhitkari School, a purpose built school, caters to the needs of young generation, by paying personalized attention to its learners. With learning by sharing method, the school ascertains its commitment to make education a meaningful and enjoyable experience. By stimulating their ‘Cords of Intellect’ the school exhorts its students to groom into considerate and compassionate individuals so that they can scale global heights by hammering out a right balance in all walks of life.

Warm Regards,
Principal

Dr. Vijay Anand

Our Mission

Our mission is prepare students spiritually, academically, and in accordance with their God-given potential, enabling them to handle the rigors of life with courage.

Our Vision

We envision a future where learners are inspired to reach their fullest potential, equipped with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Our Core Values

Integrity: We promote honesty and ethical behavior amongst our actions.

Compassion: We encourage empathy, kindness, and a spirit of service to others.

Respect: We create an environment where everyone is treated with dignity and understanding.

Truth: We value openness, transparency, and the pursuit of knowledge.

Cultural Appreciation: We honor diverse traditions and embrace the wisdom of the past while preparing for the future.

Discipline: We emphasize self-discipline and continuous personal growth.

Reasons To Join Sarvhitkari

Attentions to sports
Our school boasts excellent sports facilities, including spacious playgrounds, modern courts, and indoor arenas. Students can engage in a variety of sports such as cricket, football, and volleyball , supported by top-notch equipment and coaching. These facilities foster teamwork, physical fitness, and healthy competition.
Skilled Faculty
Our school is home to a highly skilled faculty dedicated to helping students reach their full potential. With expertise across various subjects, our teachers provide personalized guidance, fostering a supportive and engaging learning environment.
Well Equipped Lab
Our school features well-equipped labs, providing students with hands-on learning experiences and the tools they need to explore and innovate in science and technology.
Art Lab
Our school’s art lab is fully equipped with a wide range of materials and tools, providing students the perfect environment to explore their creativity. It fosters artistic growth and allows students to experiment with various mediums and techniques.

Sarvhitkari Educational Society, a state unit of Vidya Bharti, has been reshaping Punjab’s education since 1970. It operates 124 formal schools, 341 informal institutions (Sanskar Kenders), 2 Skill Development Centres, and a think tank.
