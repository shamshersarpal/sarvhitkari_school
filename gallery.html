Sarvhitkari Educational Society, a state unit of Vidya Bharti, has been reshaping Punjab’s education since 1970. It operates 124 formal schools, 341 informal institutions (Sanskar Kenders), 2 Skill Development Centres, and a think tank. The society emphasizes quality, value-based education rooted in Bhartiya culture and history to drive social transformation.
